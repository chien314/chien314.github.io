Does higher income mean longer life span?

Yes!

Are this only true for high income countries?

No!

The figure on the right shows that this indeed the case for countries in all regions in the world. One can select a particular region or all regions together select the drop-down menu on the top right corner. The slider bar on the top allows one to continuously vary the years from 1800 to 2014.

Finally, I find that high income can acatually affect the life expanctancy. I also find that this observation not only is true for developed countries but also can be generalized to all countries for different income levels in the world.

You can mouseover the circles to see the details about each country.
