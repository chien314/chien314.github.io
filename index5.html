Does higher income mean longer life span?

Yes!

Is this only true for the developed countries?

No!

The figure on the right shows that this indeed the case for countries in all regions of the world. One can select a particular region or all regions together by using the drop-down menu on the top right corner. The slider bar on the top allows one to continuously vary the year from 1800 to 2014.

Finally, I find that GDP per capita is positively correlated to the life expanctancy. I also find that this observation not only is true for developed countries but also can be generalized to all countries at different income levels in the world.

Tips:
Change the scene number on the top-left corner.
Select the region you want to look at on the top-right corner.
Move the slider on the top to adjust the year.
Mouseover the circles in the plot to see the details about each country.
