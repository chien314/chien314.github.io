Does higher income mean longer life span?

Is this only true for the developed countries?

In 2014, although Africa still has the lowest average GDP per capita and the shortest life expectancy (~60 years), the life expectancy increases by a factor of 2.3 in Africa, which is comparable to that of Europe (2.4).

So it looks like higher GDP indeed leads to longer life expectancy and this seems true for both developing and developed countries.
